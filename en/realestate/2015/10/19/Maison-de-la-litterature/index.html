Maison De La Littérature 19 Oct 2015

The Maison de la Littérature offers a unique concept in Québec City : an harmonious blend between a public library, a permanent exhibition on quebec's litterature, studio spaces and writer'S workplaces.

Located in the first Neogothic-style church in Quebec City, the house offers a stunning interior architecture, both in the purity of its white and open spaces, leaving a great deal of room for our imagination.
